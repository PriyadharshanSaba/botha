The Personal Finance
Course

This course is not for people who want to gamble with their money.

If you're looking for hot tips, overnight returns, or the next "100x coin" — this isn't your place. ABC Firm's Personal Finance Course is built for people who want to build real, lasting wealth — patiently, intelligently, and on solid foundations.

The Journey

From absolute beginner
to knowledgeable investor

No finance background needed. We take you step by step through simplified, structured financial education designed to stick — and to work in real life.

01

Financial Foundations

Understand how money actually works — budgeting, cash flow, savings rate, and breaking bad money habits.

02

Debt, Credit & Banking

Learn to use credit strategically, eliminate bad debt, and make your banking accounts work harder for you.

03

Investing Fundamentals

Mutual funds, index funds, SIPs, bonds — demystified and explained in plain language with real examples.

04

Stock Market Mastery

How equities work, reading financials, valuation basics, and building a portfolio that grows over decades.

05

Tax, Insurance & Protection

Legal tax optimisation, the right coverage, and protecting everything you've built.

06

Wealth Building Strategy

Long-term planning, real estate, retirement, and creating generational wealth on any income.

Words to live by
