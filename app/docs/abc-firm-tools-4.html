Tool 05
Tax Estimator
FY 2025–26 · New Tax Regime · Includes cess, surcharge & marginal relief
New Regime slabs (FY 2025–26): ₹0–4L → 0% · ₹4–8L → 5% · ₹8–12L → 10% · ₹12–16L → 15% · ₹16–20L → 20% · ₹20–24L → 25% · Above ₹24L → 30%. Standard deduction ₹75,000 on salary applied automatically. Rebate u/s 87A (up to ₹60,000 on taxable income ≤ ₹12L — effectively zero tax up to ₹12L) applied where eligible. 4% Health & Education Cess. Surcharge & marginal relief applied on high incomes.
Head 1 — Salaries
Head 2 — Business or Profession
Enter your net taxable profit here. If you use presumptive taxation — 44AD (business: 6–8% of turnover) or 44ADA (profession: 50% of receipts) — enter the presumptive profit amount.
Head 3 — House Property (Let-Out / Rented Property Only)
New Regime rules: Only two deductions apply — 30% standard deduction on NAV (Sec 24a) and home loan interest on let-out property (Sec 24b). However, interest deduction is capped at NAV after 30% deduction — it cannot create a loss. No set-off against other heads, no carry-forward of any HP loss. No 80C principal, 80EEA or 80EE. Self-occupied property income is always NIL — enter details here only for rented-out property.
